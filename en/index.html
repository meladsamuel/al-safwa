Welcome to
AL-SAFWA HIGH INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING

It's our pleasure to visit our institute website, and we hope that this will become beneficial to both of us. On one hand, you can share our latest news, and on the other hand we can benefit from your views and suggestions concerning the institute educational progress that keep us tuned to the requirements of the technical and technological future developments and progress.

INSTITUTE AND THE FUTURE

It's evident to all academics and scientists that the recent developments in technology and its implication on the engineering progress compelled us to develop new teaching doctrines to produce a new generation of engineers compatible with the developments in the near and far future

INSTITUTION OBJECTIVE

It has become our task to equip the current and forthcoming generations of students to develop novel engineering disciplines and knowledge to be adopted for the current and future academic departments in our institution.

STUDY DURATION

The study period at Al-Safwa institute goes in accordance with all institutions in Egypt to be primarily set to five academic years (or ten terms) after which the student is awarded the Degree of Bachelor of Science corresponding to the field of his study which is totally approved by the Ministry of Higher Education.
