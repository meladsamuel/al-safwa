Institute and future

It has become clear to all scientists that technology and engineering branches are capable of creating generations of the near and far future.

Institute’s mission

To allow the generations of students to enter the current and next era of knowledge, and to create a growing generation having knowledge in various disciplines of the Institute’s current and future departments.

Duration of study

The duration of study at the Institute is five years, after which the student will be awarded the Bachelor of Engineering degree in the specialization department, approved by the Minister of Higher Education.
