The duration of study at the Institute is five years, after which the student will be granted the Bachelor of Engineering degree in the specialization department. He is certified by the Minister of Higher Education of the Al-Kamar Society for Social Services and Development, established by Ministerial Decree No. 1116 of 2006

The Institute accepts the following qualifications:-

  • General Secondary and the equivalent of Arab and foreign certificates.
  • Secondary industrial three-year system.
  • Diploma of Industrial Technical Institutes and Technical Schools system of five years or equivalent.
  • The Institute follows the Ministry of Higher Education in all its affairs.
  • The Institute accepts transfers from the corresponding institutes and from the faculties of engineering in general, either from the preparatory division or from the higher group.
  • The Institute postpones conscription for students up to 28 years.

Technical and Technical Future of Al-Safwa Institute

The current age of knowledge requires trained engineers and the best in terms of civility, understanding and understanding of the art of engineering and its practical and applied knowledge. Therefore, the Institute seeks to link its students to universities in Europe, the United States and Canada to be a distinguished communication channel between Egypt and the world. And with an educational quality that imitates its counterparts in the outside world, and thus rehabilitates a distinguished professional who is able to meet the challenges of the global labor market. The Institute aims to reach agreements with international companies such as Microsoft, Oracle, Cisco and many other companies to train the Institute's students in the latest technologies.
