Al-Safwa Quality

Vision

Al Safwa Engineering Institute hopes to be a distinguished institutional and educational entity in the field of engineering and technology in Egypt and the Arab world. It applies the concepts of quality in the educational process, graduate studies and scientific research, continuing with civil society sectors and preserving the characteristics of its environment and region.

Mission

The preparation of a distinguished engineering graduate in the fields of engineering, technology and participation in solving the problems of the engineering and technological society, and the ability to transfer his knowledge and skill in the fields of specialization (civil engineering, architecture, electronics and communications, computer engineering).

A look that the organization wants to reach, has a strategic dimension

Developing the institutional capacity of the Institute and implementing a quality assurance system in all its components (academic, technical, administrative).

To support the educational efficiency and to update the programs and decisions and adopt the standard academic standards from the National Authority for Quality Assurance and Accreditation for higher education institutions and to develop institutional standards that reflect the graduates' specifications and characteristics of the region.

Objectives

  • Activate the clear vision of the Institute through a non-profit educational institution and the existence of a conscious leadership adopts the thought of development and participation and establish strategic planning procedures (short-term - long-term).
  • Completion and updating of the organizational structure and development of cadres (academic - technical - administrative) and consolidate the credibility, ethics and commitment among all employees of the Institute and seek to qualify all the vocabulary of the Institute to meet the requirements of academic accreditation and institutional programs of the Institute and the adequacy of faculty members and raise the level of the educational process.
  • Provide financial and material resources that establish the effective and supportive infrastructure of the educational system, improve the educational, research and technological environment, and update academic standards and programs, in accordance with professional and educational standards and expectations of beneficiaries.
  • Building a partnership in the community and supporting the communication between the Institute and the sectors of civil society and employment sectors and beneficiaries of the educational service, solving the problems of the engineering and technological community and the development of institutional capacity of the Institute (halls - laboratories - workshops - devices - requirements of the educational process) to cope with information development and increase the effectiveness of units of a nature Especially in line with changes in the environment and the labor market.
  • Building and structuring performance monitoring systems and continuous evaluation of educational effectiveness internally and externally, managing and disseminating the quality culture at the Institute and developing performance techniques and creating a positive spirit of competition between all the vocabularies of the Institute to achieve optimal performance qualified to accreditation
