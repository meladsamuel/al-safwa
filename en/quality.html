Al-Safwa Quality

VISION

Al-Safwa Engineering Institute is a distinguished institutional and educational entity in the field of engineering and technology in Egypt and in the Arab world. It applies the concepts of quality in the educational process, graduate studies and scientific research, continuing with civil society sectors and preserving the characteristics of its environment and region.

MISSION

The preparation of a distinguished engineering graduate in the fields of engineering, technology and the participation in solving problems in the engineering and technological fields as well as the ability to transfer knowledge and skills in the fields of specialization (civil engineering, architecture, electronics and communications, computer engineering).

STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE OF THE INSTITUTE

Develop the institutional capacity of the Institute and implement a quality assurance system in all its components (academic, technical and administrative).

Support the educational efficiency and update the programs and decisions making to adopt the academic standards from the National Authority for Quality Assurance and Accreditation for higher education institutions to develop institutional standards that reflect the graduates' specifications and characteristics of the region.

OBJECTIVES

  • Activate the clear vision of the Institute through a non-profitable educational institution and the existence of a conscious leadership that adopts the thought of development and participation and establish strategic planning procedures in short-term and long-term.
  • Completion and updating of the structure of the organization and development of academic, technical and administrative staff.
  • Provide financial and material resources that establish the effective and supportive infrastructure of the educational system, improve the educational, research and technological environment, and update academic program standards, in accordance with professional and educational standards and expectations of beneficiaries.
  • Building a partnership in the community and supporting the communication between the Institute and the sectors of civil society and employment sectors and benefit from the educational service, solving the problems of the engineering and technological community and the development of institutional capacity of the Institute (halls - laboratories - workshops - devices - requirements of the educational process), also to cope with information development and increase the effectiveness of units of a nature Especially in line with changes in the environment and the labor market.
  • Building and structuring performance monitoring systems and continuous evaluation of educational effectiveness internally and externally, managing and disseminating the quality culture at the Institute and developing performance techniques and creating a positive spirit of competition between all the elements of the Institute to achieve optimal performance qualified to accreditation
